User Access Onboarding Procedure (SOP)

Purpose:
Grant access to systems based on role with approval and least privilege.

Systems Covered:
- Google Workspace
- GitHub Organization
- GCP IAM
- Jira

Process:
1) Hiring manager submits access request ticket including role and required systems.
2) IT/Security approves request (must include manager approval).
3) Accounts are provisioned with role-based groups.
4) MFA enforcement confirmed before granting admin privileges.
5) Ticket is closed with timestamp and list of access granted.

Evidence Retention:
- Tickets retained for 1 year
- Quarterly spot-check on a random sample of 5 onboardings
